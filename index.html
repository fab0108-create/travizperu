The Man Behind TravizPeru

Francisco Arriz Barrantes

Private Tour Manager & Creator of TravizPeru

I was born with hospitality in my heart. Growing up in Peru, living part of my adolescence in California, and returning to fall deeply in love with my country — that journey shaped who I am today. I began my career in 1998 at iconic Lima establishments like La Rosa Náutica restaurant and Las Américas hotel. In 2004, a prestigious Peruvian tour operator recruited me to guide international groups across Peru and South America — and I never looked back. For me, this is not a job. It is my passion. Nothing makes me happier than seeing a traveler's eyes light up discovering that Peru is not just Machu Picchu — it is a thousand wonders. Come to Peru. I promise you the experience of a lifetime.

No-shows or departures after tour commencement are non-refundable. TravizPeru strongly recommends purchasing comprehensive travel insurance.

Travel Insurance

Travel insurance is strongly recommended for all TravizPeru guests. Coverage should include trip cancellation, medical evacuation, and baggage protection.

All TravizPeru escorted tours require a minimum of 10 passengers. In the event a tour does not meet the minimum, guests will be offered an alternative departure date or a full refund.

Cusco sits at 11,150 ft and Lake Titicaca at 12,500 ft above sea level. Guests with health concerns should consult their physician before booking. TravizPeru is not liable for altitude-related medical issues.
